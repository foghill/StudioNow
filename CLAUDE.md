An iOS app called “Studio Now” for WORTHLESSSTUDIOS — a not-for-profit that matches NYC artists with affordable studio spaces.
Core screens & flow:
	1.	Onboarding — Artist creates a profile: name, art discipline, portfolio link (optional).
	2.	Studio Needs Form — Artist inputs: desired square footage, preferred neighborhood(s), monthly budget, lease start date & duration, whether they’re open to sharing with co-tenants.
	3.	Match Results — List/map view of available spaces that fit their criteria. Each card shows: address, size, price, photos, co-tenant compatibility score (if applicable).
	4.	Space Detail — Full listing with photos, floor plan, amenities, lease terms, and a “Request This Space” button.
	5.	Dashboard — Shows application status, upcoming mediation sessions, rent payment schedule, and support resources.
	6.	Mediation & Support — In-app messaging or scheduling for free mediation services between co-tenants, plus a resource hub for studio setup tips.
Key features:
	∙	Matching algorithm based on size, location, budget, and timing
	∙	Co-tenant matching with compatibility indicators
	∙	Rent guarantee status tracking
	∙	Push notifications for new matches and application updates
Tech notes:
	∙	SwiftUI preferred
	∙	MapKit for neighborhood browsing
	∙	Local data/mock JSON is fine for prototype
	∙	Target iOS 17+
Brand context: WORTHLESSSTUDIOS is a not-for-profit. Tone should feel community-driven, approachable, and artist-friendly — not corporate. Think warm neutrals, clean typography, generous whitespace.
